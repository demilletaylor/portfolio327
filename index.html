Biography

I am a Visual Communication Design junior at San Francisco State University. This semester, I am planning to enroll in the SF State’s Scholars program to obtain my Master of Art in Visual Communication Design within a year of completing my Bachelor of Science.

I was born in Oakland, CA, and raised in various parts of East Bay, mainly Hayward and San Leandro. During my high school years, I lived in the Central Valley, and missed the Bay Area greatly, so I returned to attend SFSU. Besides design, my greatest passion is music. I love discovering samples, collecting vinyls, Afrofuturism, and finding Africanisms in old and modern forms of Black American music. Beside being an avid music listener, I've played piano, violin, and cello at different points during my childhood. I'm is currently teaching myself how to play the bass guitar.

Since childhood, I have consistently utilized technology to create collages, vision boards, and edit videos. However, my true passion for graphic design emerged in high school. Now, as a college student with enhanced art and design knowledge, I can confidently say that graphic design is the perfect field for me. It serves as my outlet to visually showcase ideas, capturing the essence of events, people, or organizations accurately. My favorite aspect of design is branding, especially when building something from the ground up, handpicking typography, color, texture, and overall vibe. Conceptual designs, where I can create an entire brand based on a text prompt, are my favorite projects.
